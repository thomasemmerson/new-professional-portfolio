About Me

Who am I?

Hi! My name's Tom. Tom Emmerson. Thomas David Emmerson. I'm thirty-five and I live in North Cyprus. My wife, my son and I moved here in 2019. Our son was conceived here using IVF methods back in 2016 so this is a cause and a place that’s very dear to both of us.

I’ve had a lot of jobs. I've worked in private medical insurance, technology, information technology, supermarkets, call centres, market research, hospitality, food retail, insurance underwriting.

What do I do now?

Until 2019 I'd worked in IT and software design for 8 years. I worked in medium to large enterprises, in sectors including health, insurance, regulation, food retail, cloud services and financial management.

Doing what? I worked with customers and tried to understand what they wanted. Using that information, I helped some of these businesses realign their models, processes and systems. The thing I most enjoyed doing though in all these businesses was helping design and build new things.

What do I want to do next?

I like art.

I like technology.

I am inspired by people who build beautiful things or things that do really cool jobs. I would love to do anything remotely like that. I am always looking for new opportunities, especially ones that are new or interesting. I know, it’s vague.
